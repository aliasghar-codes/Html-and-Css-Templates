Why should I work with a grid system?

When designing a website or an app, your focus should be the optimal navigation path of your users. As for the human eye geometry matters, it’s vital, that your elements are aligned proper and follow an optical vanishing line. Now with the 1440 grid you can place all your elements into rows – perfectly aligned and with optimal and even spacing between all columns. Even on widescreen layouts and drafts.
